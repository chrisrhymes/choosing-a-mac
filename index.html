Choosing a Mac

I don't know about you, but I have spent many hours looking at the Apple store trying to understand what Mac I should buy. There are lots of options available!

I've made this little web app as a way of helping you decide what option is best for you (as well as a little side project for myself to learn some more JavaScript).
